# MusicMaps-Backend
M&amp;T Meet and Hack 2nd Place

Music is the universal language and music maps intends to unite the world by connecting them through music. MusicMaps allows you to see anonymous user's music listening history and location to display music treads across the globe. Music maps allows you to add friends and keep up with their music tastes and allows for following locations to follow the top trends at any time!
